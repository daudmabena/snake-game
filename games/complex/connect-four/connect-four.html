How to Play

Click on a column to drop your disc:

  • Players take turns dropping discs into the grid
  • Discs fall to the lowest available position in the column
  • First player to connect four discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If the grid fills up with no winner, the game is a tie

Play against a friend or challenge the AI at different difficulty levels!
