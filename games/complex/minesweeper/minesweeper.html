How to Play

Uncover cells while avoiding mines:

  • Left Click: Reveal a cell
  • Right Click: Flag a potential mine
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Use logic to determine where mines are located
  • Flag all mines or reveal all safe cells to win

Choose your difficulty level or create a custom board to challenge yourself!
