How to Play

Use the keyboard to control your spaceship:

  • ← → : Rotate ship left/right
  • ↑ : Thrust forward
  • Space : Fire
  • Z : Hyperspace (teleport to a random location - risky!)

Destroy asteroids to earn points. Large asteroids break into smaller ones when hit. Watch out for UFOs that appear occasionally and shoot at you!
