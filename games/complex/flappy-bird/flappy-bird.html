How to Play

Press the spacebar, click, or tap to control the bird:

  • Space / Click / Tap: Make the bird flap upward
  • Navigate through the gaps between pipes
  • Each pipe you pass earns you a point
  • Don't hit the pipes or the ground!

The game gets progressively more challenging as your score increases. How far can you go?
