How to Play

Watch and repeat the sequence of colors:

  • Simon will show a sequence of colors
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colors in the same order
  • Each round adds one more color to the sequence
  • Make a mistake and you'll have to start over (or from the beginning in strict mode)

How far can you go? Test your memory and see if you can beat your high score!
