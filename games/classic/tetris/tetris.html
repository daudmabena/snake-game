How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move and rotate the falling pieces:

  • ← → : Move left/right
  • ↑ : Rotate piece
  • ↓ : Soft drop
  • Space : Hard drop

Complete horizontal lines to score points and prevent the pieces from stacking to the top!
