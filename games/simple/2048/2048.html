How to Play

Use the arrow keys to slide numbered tiles on the grid:

  • ← → ↑ ↓ : Slide tiles in that direction
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  • Create a tile with the number 2048 to win
  • If the grid fills up with no possible moves, the game is over

Plan your moves carefully to reach higher numbers and achieve a high score!
