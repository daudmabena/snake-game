How to Play

Guess the hidden word one letter at a time:

  • Click on letters to make guesses
  • Each incorrect guess adds a part to the hangman
  • 6 incorrect guesses and you lose
  • Guess the word before the hangman is complete to win

Choose different categories for variety, and use hints if you get stuck!
