How to Play

Click or tap on the moles as they appear from their holes:

  • Each successful hit earns you points
  • The game lasts for 30 seconds
  • Moles appear faster as the difficulty increases
  • Try to get the highest score possible before time runs out!

Be quick! The moles don't stay up for long!
