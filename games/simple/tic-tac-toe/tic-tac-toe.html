How to Play

Click on an empty cell to place your mark (X or O):

  • Players take turns placing their marks
  • First player to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all cells are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a tie

Choose between playing against another player or against the AI with different difficulty levels.
